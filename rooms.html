Deluxe Rooms

The Deluxe rooms at Luxury Hotel offer exceptional comfort with elegant design and modern amenities. Each room comes with a spacious living area and stunning views.
There are 3 types of Deluxe rooms in this hotel: Deluxe Basic, Deluxe Common, and Deluxe Rare

Deluxe Basic

  • Room Size: 27 sqm
  • Type of Bed: 1 Queen Bed
  • Facilities: Bed, smart TV, free wi-fi, bathroom, workbench, sofa, minibar, spacious storage cupboard, and meeting room

Enjoy spectacular city views from the Deluxe Basic room, featuring a luxurious Queen bed that ensures maximum comfort, along with adapted amenities in the 27 sqm room.

Deluxe Common

  • Room Size: 30 sqm
  • Type of Bed: 1 Queen Bed
  • Facilities: Bed, smart TV, free wi-fi, bathroom, workbench, sofa, minibar, spacious storage cupboard, and meeting room

Within the Deluxe Common room, you will find ample space and a comfortable Queen bed, perfect for accommodating your small family.

Enjoy panoramic views of Jakarta in this 54 sqm suite, complete with a relaxing bathtub to ensure your relaxation and enjoyment

Deluxe Rare

  • Room Size: 76 sqm

  • Facilities: Bed, smart TV, free wi-fi, bathroom, workbench, sofa, minibar, spacious storage cupboard, and meeting room

The room designed for two people with a unique King bed is equipped with a very luxurious view, specially arranged room to enjoy comfort for longer

Executive Epic
