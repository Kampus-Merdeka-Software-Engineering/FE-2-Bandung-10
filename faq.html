FAQ
  • What facilities guests have access and include in the room rate?

    We provide free Wi-Fi, swimming pool, on-site restaurant and gym. However, breakfast is not included in the room price

  • What time to Check-In & Check-Out?

    Our check-in time starts at 14:00 PM, while check-out time is 12:00 PM. We also offer early check-in options and late check-out options at an additional charge, subject to room availability

  • Does the hotel provide parking and valet parking facilities and is there any additional charge?

    Of course, this parking facility can be used without additional costs. If you prefer valet parking, this service is also available for an additional fee which you can obtain at the front desk

  • What is the reservation cancellation policy at this hotel?

    We provide free cancellation if done 1 day before the check-in date. After that, a cancellation fee of IDR 250,000 will be charged
